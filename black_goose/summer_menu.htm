Hummus - חומוס

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Garbanzo Beans (soaked overnight)
  • 1/2 Cup of Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • 3/4 Cup of Tahini Paste
  • Lemon Juice
  • Minced Garlic
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • 1 tbsp. of Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Drain the garbanzo beans and rinse
  2. Put into large pot, add water and cover
  3. Bring to boil then simmer for 2 hours
  4. Blend garbanzo beans
  5. Slowly add olive oil to the mix
  6. Add tahini and lemon juice
  7. Add garlic, cumin and salt
  8. Spread Hummus into bowl
  9. Drizzle olive oil and paprika
